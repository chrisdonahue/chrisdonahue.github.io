I am a PhD candidate at the University of California, San Diego studying computer music. I am co-advised by Miller Puckette (music) and Julian McAuley (computer science). My main research focus is the application of machine learning to audio signal processing and music information retrieval.

In 2013 I received a BS in computer science from the Turing Scholars Program at the University of Texas at Austin. In 2016 I received an MA in computer music from UCSD.
